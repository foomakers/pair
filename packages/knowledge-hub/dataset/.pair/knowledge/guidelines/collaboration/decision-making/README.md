# Decision Making Framework

## Strategic Overview

This framework establishes systematic decision-making excellence through structured analysis, collaborative evaluation, and strategic judgment that ensures optimal outcomes, stakeholder alignment, and organizational value across all decision contexts and complexity levels.

## Core Decision Architecture

### Universal Decision Orchestrator

The **DecisionMakingOrchestrator** provides comprehensive decision management through specialized engines:

- **AnalysisEngine**: Information gathering, option evaluation, and impact assessment
- **CollaborationEngine**: Stakeholder engagement, perspective integration, and consensus building
- **EvaluationEngine**: Criteria application, risk assessment, and outcome prediction
- **AuthorityEngine**: Decision rights, approval workflows, and accountability management
- **ImplementationEngine**: Execution planning, resource allocation, and progress monitoring
- **LearningEngine**: Outcome tracking, lesson capture, and process improvement
