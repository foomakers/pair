# Review Processes Framework

## Strategic Overview

This framework establishes systematic review excellence through structured evaluation, collaborative validation, and continuous improvement that ensures quality assurance, knowledge sharing, and organizational learning across all deliverables and decision-making processes.

## Core Review Architecture

### Universal Review Orchestrator

The **ReviewProcessOrchestrator** provides comprehensive review management through specialized engines:

- **AssignmentEngine**: Reviewer selection, expertise matching, and workload optimization
- **EvaluationEngine**: Quality assessment, criteria validation, and scoring management
- **CollaborationEngine**: Multi-reviewer coordination, consensus building, and conflict resolution
- **FeedbackEngine**: Comment management, suggestion tracking, and improvement coordination
- **ApprovalEngine**: Decision workflow, authority validation, and sign-off management
- **LearningEngine**: Knowledge capture, pattern recognition, and process improvement
