## Quick Reference

### Common Roles

- **Product Manager**: Strategic vision, requirements, and stakeholder management
- **Product Engineer**: Full-stack development with product mindset
- **Staff Engineer**: Technical leadership, architecture, and mentoring
- **DevOps Engineer**: Infrastructure, deployment, and operational excellence
- **QA Engineer**: Quality assurance, testing, and validation processes

### Responsibility Areas

- **Strategic**: Vision setting, roadmap planning, stakeholder management
- **Tactical**: Sprint planning, task breakdown, execution coordination
- **Operational**: Implementation, testing, deployment, maintenance
- **Quality**: Code review, testing, documentation, compliance

### Common Patterns

- **Single Owner**: Clear accountability for specific deliverables
- **Shared Responsibility**: Collaborative ownership with defined boundaries
- **Review Chain**: Multi-stage approval process with clear criteria
- **Escalation Path**: Clear escalation procedures for conflicts or blockers

## Best Practices

### Role Definition

1. **Clear Boundaries**: Define what each role is and isn't responsible for
2. **Measurable Outcomes**: Establish measurable success criteria for each role
3. **Decision Authority**: Clearly define decision-making authority and scope
4. **Escalation Paths**: Provide clear escalation procedures for conflicts

### Team Dynamics

1. **Regular Reviews**: Periodic review and refinement of role effectiveness
2. **Feedback Culture**: Encourage open feedback on role clarity and effectiveness
3. **Flexibility**: Allow role evolution as team and project needs change
4. **Documentation**: Maintain current documentation of role definitions and changes

### Implementation

1. **Gradual Introduction**: Implement role changes gradually to allow adaptation
2. **Training Support**: Provide training for new or evolving role responsibilities
3. **Tool Integration**: Ensure role definitions integrate with collaboration tools
4. **Success Metrics**: Track role effectiveness through clear metrics and indicators
