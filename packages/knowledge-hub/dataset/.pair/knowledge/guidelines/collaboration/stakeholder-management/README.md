# Stakeholder Management Framework

## Strategic Overview

This framework establishes systematic stakeholder management excellence through intelligent relationship building, strategic engagement, and value alignment that ensures project success, organizational support, and sustainable collaboration across all stakeholder levels and interest domains.

## Core Stakeholder Architecture

### Universal Stakeholder Orchestrator

The **StakeholderManagementOrchestrator** provides comprehensive relationship management through specialized engines:

- **IdentificationEngine**: Stakeholder mapping, influence analysis, and relationship discovery
- **AnalysisEngine**: Interest assessment, power evaluation, and engagement strategy development
- **EngagementEngine**: Communication optimization, interaction management, and relationship building
- **AlignmentEngine**: Expectation management, consensus building, and value coordination
- **InfluenceEngine**: Persuasion strategies, coalition building, and support cultivation
- **MonitoringEngine**: Relationship tracking, satisfaction measurement, and engagement effectiveness
