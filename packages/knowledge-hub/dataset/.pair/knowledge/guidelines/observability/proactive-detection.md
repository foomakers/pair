# Proactive Detection

## Purpose

Define strategies for early detection of system issues through predictive monitoring, trend analysis, and intelligent alerting to prevent problems before they impact users.

## Scope

**In Scope:**
- Predictive monitoring strategies
- Early warning system design
- Trend analysis and forecasting
- Anomaly detection techniques
- Automated response systems
- Preventive maintenance approaches

**Out of Scope:**
- Reactive incident response procedures
- Manual troubleshooting techniques
- Post-incident analysis processes
- Business continuity planning

## Introduction

Proactive detection shifts from reactive problem-solving to predictive issue prevention. By identifying patterns, trends, and early warning signals, teams can address potential problems before they escalate into user-impacting incidents.
