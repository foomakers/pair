# Markdown Templates for Content Strategy

Structured markdown templates for consistent content creation and formatting.

## Usage in Content Strategy

- **Article Template Structure**: Standardized format for help documentation
- **Content Formatting**: Consistent structure with overview, prerequisites, steps
- **Template Components**: Headers, troubleshooting sections, next steps
- **Metadata Integration**: Last updated dates, feedback links

## Article Template

```markdown
# Article Title Template

## Overview
Brief summary of what this article covers and who it's for.

## Prerequisites
- Required knowledge or setup
- Access levels needed
- Links to foundational content

## Step-by-Step Instructions
### Step 1: [Action Title]
- **What to do**: Specific action
- **Where to find it**: Interface location
- **Expected result**: What should happen

## Troubleshooting
Common issues and solutions

## Next Steps
Related actions and advanced topics
```

_Ensures consistent content structure and user experience across documentation._
