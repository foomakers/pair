# Adoption & Guidelines (Supporting Subdomain)

> Classification: **Supporting**

**Business Purpose:**
Manage the adoption of practices, tools, and processes, and define operational and technical guidelines for the team.

**Key Capabilities:**

- Track and validate adopted practices and tools
- Develop and maintain guidelines for development and collaboration
- Ensure alignment with team standards and project goals

**Strategic Importance:**
Enables operational efficiency and standardization, supporting core subdomains.

**Complexity Assessment:**
Medium complexity; requires ongoing updates and team alignment.

**Data Ownership:**
Adoption records, guidelines documents, validation logs.

**Dependencies:**

- Depends on: Collaborative Workflow
- Provides to: How To Knowledge, Code & Documentation Generation

**Team Recommendations:**
Process owners, technical leads, and team facilitators.

**Implementation Priority:**
Medium; essential for scaling and maintaining quality.
