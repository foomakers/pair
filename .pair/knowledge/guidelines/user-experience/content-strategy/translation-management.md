# Translation Management

## Introduction

Translation management encompasses the processes, tools, and strategies for managing multilingual content across user interfaces and digital products. This includes workflow optimization, quality assurance, localization coordination, and the integration of translation activities with design and development processes to ensure consistent, high-quality user experiences across languages and cultures.

## Scope

### In Scope

- Translation workflow design and optimization
- Translation quality assurance and validation
- Localization project management
- Translation memory and terminology management
- Cultural adaptation and internationalization
- Translation tool integration and automation
- Cross-cultural user experience considerations
- Multilingual content governance
- Translation performance measurement
- Global release coordination

### Out of Scope

- Professional translation services provision
- Language learning and education
- Cultural consulting and strategy
- Legal compliance for international markets
- International business development
