# Welcome to pair

**Code is the easy part.** pair helps development teams collaborate with AI assistants throughout the product lifecycle.

## What is pair?

pair is a suite of resources and workflows designed to guide and enable collaboration between development teams and AI assistants. It provides tools for defining epics, stories, guidelines, and adoptions, integrating with major code assistants and project management tools.
